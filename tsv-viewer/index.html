Yes. Upload your TSV file and it will render as a searchable, sortable table while preserving tab-delimited structure.

Is this the right tool for comma-separated CSV files?

No. For comma-delimited files, use CSV Viewer Online. This page is optimized specifically for TSV inputs.

Can I inspect TSV exports without changing delimiters?

Yes. The workflow is designed for inspection and validation, so you can review data without converting it to CSV first.
